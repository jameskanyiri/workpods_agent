# Payroll Filing Deadlines

## Monthly Returns

| Return | Authority | Deadline | Sinopsis |
|--------|-----------|----------|----------|
| PAYE (P10) | KRA | 9th of following month | Remit tax deducted from employee salaries |
| NHIF | NHIF | 9th of following month | Remit employee NHIF contributions |
| NSSF | NSSF | 15th of following month | Remit employee + employer NSSF contributions |
| Housing Levy | KRA | 9th of following month | Remit employee + employer housing levy |
| NITA | NITA | By end of following month | Remit employer NITA levy (KES 50/employee) |

## Annual Returns

| Return | Authority | Deadline | Description |
|--------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| P9A Tax Deduction Cards | KRA | By end of February | Annual summary of each employee's PAYE deductions |
| P10A Annual PAYE Summary | KRA | By end of February | Employer's annual summary of all PAYE remitted |
| Employment Income Return | KRA | 30th June | Included in annual corporate tax return |

## Late Filing Penalties

| Return | Penalty |
|--------|---------|
| PAYE | 25% of tax due or KES 10,000, whichever is higher, plus 2% monthly interest |
| NSSF | 5% penalty per month on outstanding amount |
| NHIF | 5x the contribution amount |
| Housing Levy | 3% penalty per month on outstanding amount |
| NITA | Penalties per NITA Act regulations |

## System Alert Schedule

The system generates proactive alerts to prevent missed deadlines:

| Alert | Trigger Date | Message |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| PAYE reminder | 5th of month | "PAYE P10 due in 4 days" |
| NHIF reminder | 5th of month | "NHIF return due in 4 days" |
| NSSF reminder | 10th of month | "NSSF return due in 5 days" |
| Housing Levy reminder | 5th of month | "Housing Levy due in 4 days" |
| P9A reminder | 1st February | "Annual P9A cards due by end of February" |

## Notes

- If a deadline falls on a weekend or public holiday, the return is due on the last business day before the deadline
- Penalties accrue automatically; early filing is strongly recommended
- The system tracks filing status per period and flags any unfiled returns
