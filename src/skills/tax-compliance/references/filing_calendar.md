# KRA Filing Calendar

## Monthly Filings

| Return | Deadline | Authority | Description |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| PAYE (P10) | 9th | KRA | Employee income tax deductions |
| VAT-3 | 20th | KRA | Monthly VAT return |
| WHT | 20th | KRA | Withholding tax on vendor payments |
| NHIF | 9th | NHIF | Employee health insurance contributions |
| NSSF | 15th | NSSF | Employee + employer pension contributions |
| Housing Levy | 9th | KRA | Affordable housing levy |
| Excise Duty | 20th | KRA | If applicable to business activities |

## Quarterly Filings

| Return | Deadline | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Instalment Tax (1st) | 20th April | 25% of estimated annual tax |
| Instalment Tax (2nd) | 20th June | 25% of estimated annual tax |
| Instalment Tax (3rd) | 20th September | 25% of estimated annual tax |
| Instalment Tax (4th) | 20th December | 25% of estimated annual tax |

## Annual Filings

| Return | Deadline | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Corporate Tax Return | 6 months after year-end | Annual income tax return |
| P9A Tax Deduction Cards | End of February | Employee annual PAYE summary |
| P10A Annual PAYE | End of February | Employer annual PAYE summary |
| Annual VAT Summary | With corporate return | Annual VAT reconciliation |

## Penalties for Late Filing

| Return | Late Filing Penalty | Late Payment Interest |
|--------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| PAYE | KES 10,000 or 25% of tax (higher) | 2% per month |
| VAT | KES 10,000 or 5% of tax (higher) | 2% per month |
| WHT | 5% of WHT amount | 1% per month |
| Corporate Tax | 5% of tax due | 2% per month |
| Instalment Tax | 20% of shortfall (if underpaid by >10%) | 2% per month |

## System Alert Schedule

The system generates alerts before each deadline:

| Days Before Deadline | Alert Level | Action |
|---------------------|-------------|--------|
| 5 days | Info | Reminder notification |
| 2 days | Warning | Escalation to accountant |
| 0 days (due date) | Critical | Urgent notification to owner |
| Past due | Alert | Daily reminders until filed |

## Key Dates Quick Reference

```
Monthly cycle:
  5th  - PAYE/NHIF/Housing Levy alert
  9th  - PAYE, NHIF, Housing Levy DUE
  10th - NSSF alert
  15th - NSSF DUE, VAT/WHT alert
  20th - VAT-3, WHT DUE

Quarterly (instalment tax):
  15th Apr/Jun/Sep/Dec - Alert
  20th Apr/Jun/Sep/Dec - Instalment tax DUE

Annual:
  1st Feb  - P9A/P10A alert
  28th Feb - P9A/P10A DUE
  Corporate return - 6 months after financial year end
```

## Notes

- If a deadline falls on a weekend or public holiday, the due date is the last preceding business day
- KRA may grant extensions in writing for specific returns; these are rare
- Instalment tax shortfall penalty applies if total instalments are less than 110% of prior year tax or less than actual current year liability
