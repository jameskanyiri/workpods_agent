# VAT Rates and Categories

## Rate Categories

### Standard Rate: 16%

Applies to most goods and services supplied in Kenya that are not specifically zero-rated or exempt.

### Zero-Rated: 0%

VAT is charged at 0%, but the supplier can still claim input VAT on purchases.

**Zero-rated items include:**
- Exports of goods and services
- Basic foodstuffs: maize flour, wheat flour, cassava flour, rice, bread
- Agricultural pest control products
- Supplies to Export Processing Zones (EPZ)
- International transport services
- Crude oil and other petroleum products (some categories)

### Exempt

No VAT is charged, and the supplier cannot claim input VAT on related purchases.

**Exempt supplies include:**
- Financial services (banking, insurance premiums, interest)
- Education services (registered institutions)
- Healthcare services (registered facilities)
- Unprocessed agricultural products (fresh fruits, vegetables, milk, eggs, meat)
- Residential rent
- Public transport services (fare-paying passengers)
- Sale or transfer of going concern

## VAT Computation

```
Output VAT = Sum of VAT on all sales invoices (standard-rated only)
Input VAT  = Sum of VAT on all purchase invoices (standard-rated only)
VAT-3 Liability = Output VAT - Input VAT
```

- If liability is positive: VAT is payable to KRA
- If liability is negative: VAT refund is claimable (subject to KRA verification)

## Input VAT Rules

- Input VAT is only claimable on purchases used to make taxable supplies
- Apportionment applies if purchases relate to both taxable and exempt supplies
- Input VAT on entertainment is not deductible (except for entertainment businesses)
- Tax invoices must contain valid KRA PIN, eTIMS control number, and VAT registration number

## VAT-3 Return

The monthly VAT-3 return includes:
- Total standard-rated sales and VAT thereon
- Total zero-rated sales
- Total exempt sales
- Total purchases and input VAT claimed
- Net VAT payable or refundable

## Notes

- All businesses with annual turnover above KES 5,000,000 must register for VAT
- Voluntary registration is allowed for businesses below the threshold
- VAT registration grants an obligation to file monthly VAT-3 returns even if nil
